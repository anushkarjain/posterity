Give a helping hand to those who need it

We aim at bridging the intergenerational gap while enculcating good habits in kids. Kids(14+) can take up the tasks old people need help with.

Helps Bridge generational gap

Completing the task will encourage children to connect with older generation and spend time with them.

Get rewards

For every task, rewards will be given in retrun, either monetary or non-monetary, this will encourage children into doing more tasks.

Helps old people overcome loneliness

Since old people can spend time with kids doing fun activities, it'll help them to terminate loneliness.

Makes kids independent and responsible

since, kids will be assigned tasks and deadlines, they'll grow into more disciplined, responsible and kind-hearted adults.
